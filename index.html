about me

I am a leading Front End Developer with experience in applying deep technical knowledge and creative design sense to create stunning and functinoal websites. I combine sound technical frameworks with appealing media assets to generate front end solutions focused on enhancing user experience. I'm capable of highly organized multitasking with expertise in writing CSS, developing original designs, and project management.

I began my journey with coding and web development in high school. At my first job I was tasked with updating an ancient Sharepoint site using HTML and CSS. Being a kid born in the mid 90's I had experience editing preexisting HTML in an attempt to make my MySpace or Tumblr page the most unique thing amongst my those of my friend group. Needless to say something about coding and web development has stuck with me all this time. While I was in college I fell in love with design volunteering for the TV station on campus. I desginded everything from flyers and social media graphics for our marketing department to web banners for our website. Once I graduated I had the opportunity to bring my love of coding and my love of design together with my first contract job. In that position I was tasked with leading the design and development of an internal micro-site meant for exclusive members of their website, or for their sales team as a means of keeping an inventory of specific brands of product. From there I began a freelance career designing and developing various web sites and web applications for a number of businesses. I've expanded my coding knowledge far beyond just HTML and CSS and have even started developing projects of my own outside of my professional work.
